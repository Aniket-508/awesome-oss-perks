## Perks

- **Free Pro Plan**: Full access to the Pro plan at no cost (normally $300/month) for qualifying non-commercial open source projects.
- **Custom Domains & Branding**: Present documentation under your own domain with custom branding and styling.
- **Advanced Analytics**: Understand how users interact with your documentation and identify improvement opportunities.
- **API Playground**: Interactive API documentation with live testing capabilities for your endpoints.
- **AI Chat & Version Control**: Pro features including AI chat, version control, and advanced components for world-class documentation.

## Eligibility

- Open source with a recognized OSS license (MIT, Apache 2.0, GPL, etc.).
- Not venture-backed or revenue-funded.
- Not owned or primarily maintained by a for-profit company.

## How to apply

1. Apply through the Mintlify OSS Program page.
2. Mintlify reviews the application for eligibility.
