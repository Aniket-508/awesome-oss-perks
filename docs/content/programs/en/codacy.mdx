## Perks

- **Free Team Plan**: Full cloud platform access at no cost for open source projects, including team dashboards, quality gates, and PR integration.
- **Security Scanning**: SAST, secrets detection, and dependency vulnerability scanning across all 49 supported languages.
- **Code Coverage Tracking**: Track test coverage metrics with inline reporting and coverage gates on pull requests.

## Eligibility

- Must be an open source project with a public repository.

## How to apply

1. Sign up at codacy.com with your GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket account.
2. Open source repositories are automatically eligible for the free Team plan.
