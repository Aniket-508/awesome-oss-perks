- **Provider**: CircleCI
- **Category**: ci-cd
- **Website**: [https://circleci.com/open-source/](https://circleci.com/open-source/)

## Perks

- **400,000 Linux Credits/Month**: Up to 400,000 credits per month for Linux, Arm, and Docker builds.
- **30,000 macOS/Windows Credits**: 30,000 credits per month for macOS and Windows builds.

## Eligibility

- Must be on CircleCI's Free Plan.
- Repository must be public.

## Requirements

- Enable the "Only build pull requests" setting to reduce unnecessary builds.

## How to apply

1. Sign up for CircleCI's Free Plan.
2. Keep your repository public to automatically qualify for open-source credits.
