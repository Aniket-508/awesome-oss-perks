- **Provider**: BrowserStack
- **Category**: testing
- **Duration**: Lifetime membership
- **Website**: [https://www.browserstack.com/open-source](https://www.browserstack.com/open-source)

## Perks

- **Unlimited Testing**: Unlimited testing across 3,000+ browsers and real mobile devices.
- **All BrowserStack Products**: Access to Live, Automate, Percy, App Live, Screenshots, and App Automate.
- **5 Concurrent Users**: 5 concurrent users and 5 parallels for faster testing and team collaboration.

## Eligibility

- Must be an open-source project (web frameworks, libraries, web applications, or mobile apps).

## How to apply

1. Sign up or log in on the BrowserStack open-source program page.
