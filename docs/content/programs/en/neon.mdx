- **Provider**: Neon
- **Category**: infrastructure
- **Duration**: Annual (renewable)
- **Website**: [https://neon.com/programs/open-source](https://neon.com/programs/open-source)

## Perks

- **Annual Credits**: Up to $5,000 in Neon credits per year for serverless Postgres databases.
- **Cash Referral Payouts**: $20 per developer who becomes a Neon customer through your referral link via GitHub Sponsorships.
- **Promotional Support**: Exposure to Neon's 1M+ developer community through co-marketing and promotional campaigns.

## Eligibility

- Project must use Postgres as a core component (database, extension, plugin, or workflow).
- Must be publicly available under a recognized open source license and self-hostable.
- Must provide documentation showing how to build with your software and Neon.

## How to apply

1. Apply through the Neon Open Source Program page.
2. Neon typically responds within 1–3 business days.
3. After the first year, projects are reviewed for renewal based on progress and credit usage.
